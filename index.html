Introduction to Climate Action

Climate action refers to the collective and individual efforts to address the challenges posed by climate change. It encompasses reducing carbon emissions, adapting to climate risks, and transitioning to renewable energy sources.

These efforts range from global agreements like the Paris Agreement to local community-based initiatives for sustainability.
