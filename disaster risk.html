Disaster Risk Reduction

Prevention

Objective:

To eliminate or reduce the chances of hazards leading to disasters.

Key Activities:

(i) Land-use planning and zoning to avoid high-risk areas.


Preparedness

Objective:

To ensure readiness to respond to disasters effectively.

Key Activities:

(i) Developing and practicing emergency response plans.


Response

Objective:

To minimize the immediate impacts of a disaster and provide relief.

Key Activities:

(i) Search and rescue operations.
