Climate Literacy Programs

School-Based Climate Education Programs

Curriculum Integration: Embedding climate science, sustainability, and environmental studies into existing school curricula at all levels.

Project-Based Learning: Encouraging students to engage in hands-on activities such as energy audits, planting trees, or creating climate action projects.


Community Outreach and Public Awareness Campaigns

Workshops and Seminars: Organizing community events to explain the basics of climate change and its local impacts.

Media Campaigns: Using social media, radio, TV, and print materials to spread climate awareness.


Youth-Focused Climate Initiatives

Youth Climate Clubs: Supporting young people in organizing environmental groups in schools or communities.

Eco-Challenges and Competitions: Encouraging youth participation in contests focused on sustainability projects or innovative climate solutions.
