Water Management

Water Supply Management

Objective:

To ensure the availability of water for domestic, industrial, and agricultural use.

Key Activities:

(i) Rainwater harvesting systems.


Water Demand Management

Objective:

To reduce water consumption and optimize usage.

Key Activities:

(i) Educating the public about water-saving practices.


Irrigation Management

Objective:

To optimize water use in agriculture, which is the largest consumer of water globally.

Key Activities:

(i) Adopting water-efficient crop varieties.
