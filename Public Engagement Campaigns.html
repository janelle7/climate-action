Public Engagement Campaigns

Awareness Campaigns

Purpose: To educate the public about a specific issue, cause, or event.

Example: Public health campaigns (e.g., anti-smoking or mental health awareness)

Channels: Social media, TV/radio ads


Advocacy Campaigns

Purpose: To mobilize public support and influence policy or decision-making.

Example: Grassroots movements urging government action.

Channels: Petitions, rallies


Behavior Change Campaigns

Purpose: To encourage individuals or communities to adopt healthier, safer, or more sustainable behaviors.

Example: Recycling campaigns.

Channels: Educational workshops, public service announcements
