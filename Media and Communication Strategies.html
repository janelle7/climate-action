Media and Communication Strategies

Public Relations (PR) Strategy

Purpose: To manage the organization's image and build positive relationships with the public and stakeholders.

Example: Press releases, media briefings, and news conferences.

Channels: News media, blogs


Social Media Strategy

Purpose: To engage audiences through platforms like Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, LinkedIn, and TikTok.

Example: Content calendars for consistent posting.

Key Approaches: Using visuals, hashtags


Storytelling and Personal Narratives

Purpose: Humanize climate change impacts by sharing personal stories.

Example: Highlighting farmers affected by droughts.

Channels: Films, blogs, social media posts
