Sustainable Transportation

Active Transportation

Benefits:

(i) Zero emissions and low environmental impact.

(ii) Promotes physical health and reduces traffic congestion.

Examples:

(i) Bicycle-sharing programs.

(ii) Dedicated pedestrian and cycling paths.


Public Transportation

Benefits:

(i) Cost-effective and accessible for diverse populations.

(ii) Can be powered by renewable energy sources like electricity and biofuels.

Examples:

(i) Electrified train systems.

(ii) Bus Rapid Transit (BRT) networks.


Low-Emission and Zero-Emission Vehicles

Benefits:

(i) Reduces dependence on fossil fuels.

(ii) Technological advancements enable greater range and efficiency.

Examples:

(i) Electric buses and taxis.

(ii) Hydrogen-powered trucks.
