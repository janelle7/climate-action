Coastal Zone Management

Integrated Coastal Zone Management (ICZM)

Definition:

(i) A holistic approach that integrates all aspects of coastal development, ecosystems, and stakeholders to achieve sustainable development.

Example:

(i) Implementation of ICZM plans to protect biodiversity while promoting tourism.


Adaptive Coastal Management

Definition:

(i) A flexible, iterative approach that adjusts policies and actions based on new data, changes in environmental conditions, or outcomes.

Example:

(i) Adjusting shoreline protection strategies as erosion rates change.


Community-Based Coastal Management

Definition:

(i) Coastal management where local communities are actively involved in decision-making and implementation.

Example:

(i) Local cooperatives managing mangrove forests to sustain fisheries.
